About

Wine is made from grapes and yet no other libation delivers such a range of complex colors, aromas, and flavors. The presence of this historic and cultural beverage has endured for centuries through religion and tradition. Geloso Wine Bar is here to enrich your life through the experience of wine, platters and conversation. Be experimental, learn as you savor, and let us bring the charm of wine into your life!

Our passion for wine in combination with a warm, relaxing enviroment, promise to offer you a unique wine experience. Choose between a variety of Cypriot, Greek and Italian wine and please yourself with quality italian platter. Either you are looking for a chillout summer night or for a place to enjoy a glass of wine with good friend, geloso wine bar is the place to be.

Sometimes wine is just necessary!
